Stawberry Banana Breeze Smoothie

<-Back to home

Description

The perfect drink to sip while laying poolside or sitting on the porch on a sunny day. Use frozen fruit for best results

Ingredients

  • 1 medium banana
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh strawberries
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup pineapple
  • 1 tablesoon sliced almonds (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon flax seeds(Optional)

Steps

  1. Puree all ingredients in a blender until smooth.
