A Delicious OG Mac n Cheese Recipe

<-Back to home

Description

This gooey, creamy, cheesy Mac n Cheese will have you feeling like you're in heaven with every bite. With just 5 simple household ingredients, you will be able to make a dish that will stun any guests, and guess what. It only takes 10 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces elbow maccaroni
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup Country Crock Spread
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in large pot according to package directions.
  2. Meanwhile, whisk milk, Country CrockÂ® Spread, and flour in large saucepan. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, whisking frequently. Reduce heat and cook, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 5 minutes. Stir in cheese until melted; remove from heat. Drain pasta and fold into the sauce.
